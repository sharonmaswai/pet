The cat ate my homework

My cat Penny has this thing where it gets excited with a single object and for the next couple of hours it will focus solely on that object. This time the focus of Penny's attention was my assignment. I swear that cat does not take anything else other than cat food so I can't understand how it developed an appetite for paper.

What I can say is that my assignment is sitting in a mound of cat refuse and I cannot seem to find an excuse to give my lecturer.

Here are some of the things I may say:

  • The dog ate my homework (a classic)
  • The cat ate my homework.
  • I misplace the assignment.
